Breathing Exercises

Sit or lie down comfortably.
Place one hand on your chest and the other on your abdomen.
Inhale deeply through your nose, letting your abdomen rise while keeping your chest relatively still.
Exhale slowly through your mouth, letting your abdomen fall.
Repeat several times, focusing on the sensation of your breath.
Inhale through your nose for 4 seconds.
Hold your breath for 7 seconds.
Exhale completely through your mouth for 8 seconds.
Repeat for 4 breath cycles.
Inhale through your nose for 4 seconds.
Hold your breath for 4 seconds.
Exhale completely through your mouth for 4 seconds.
Hold your breath for 4 seconds.
Repeat for 8 breath cycles.
Focus on the sensation of your breath.
Inhale deeply through your nose.
Exhale slowly through your mouth.
If your mind wanders, gently bring it back to the sensation of your breath.
Continue for 5-10 minutes.
Inhale deeply through your nose while counting to 4.
Exhale slowly through your mouth while counting to 6 or 8.
Repeat for several breath cycles focusing on extending the exhale.
Find a comfortable place to sit or lie down.
Inhale and tense your toes for 5 seconds.
Exhale and relax your toes.
Work your way up your body, tensing and relaxing each muscle group.
Finish with your face and head.
